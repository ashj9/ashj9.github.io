More about my research projects:

I am working on semantic segmentation task on the outdoor scenes. Our major interests is in segmentation of power line cables and towers for their inspection and maintenance. With the lack of precisely labelled data for using deep learning based solutions, we are using statistical properties for segmentation.

As part of my internship, I got a chance to work on reconstruction of textureless surfaces for outdoor objects. The major objects of interest were bridge pillars, railway tracks , icebergs etc. I have explored the limitations of simple SfM based approaches and my research involves use of marching methods for the reconstruction of the textureless scenes. You must have been responded to someone who just whispered your name even in a noisy place, right? That's what we term the 'Cocktail Party Effect'. It is our ability to focus one's auditory attention on a particular stimulus while filtering out a range of other stimuli. Do our other organs have this amazing property?

In this project, we studied the presence of 'Visual Cocktail party' effect.
